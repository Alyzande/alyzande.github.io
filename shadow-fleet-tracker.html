Shadow Fleet Tracker — Maritime Intelligence Platform

Year: 2025

Technologies: Python, Flask/FastAPI, GeoPandas, Leaflet.js, JavaScript, WebSockets, AISStream.io, TeleGeography GeoJSON

Overview

The Shadow Fleet Tracker is a real-time maritime intelligence platform that detects and visualises suspicious vessel activity worldwide using AIS (Automatic Identification System) data. It identifies vessels operating under flags of convenience or loitering near critical undersea infrastructure such as submarine cables.

Key Features

  • Real-time AIS data ingestion and anomaly detection.
  • Geofencing alerts for proximity to high-value maritime zones.
  • Interactive Leaflet.js dashboard with live vessel tracking.
  • Integration-ready architecture for satellite and sanctions datasets.
